About

From Web Layout to Game design, I love the art of coding. Since Childhood I've dreamed about learning how to code, so that one day I could make an indie game that people would remember me by, And I'm in the progress of making that dream come through. Even if I have to go through the ranks, the dream will push me through any problems or hardships I encounter.

On the way I've learned how to make useful websites with knowledge and experience with both front-end and back-end coding. I've made a couple of simple games with many in the works. I've also held a leadership role for two years, enjoying teaching new people the skills that I acquired. I hope that I will be given more opportunities like that in the future.

Even Now the dream goes on, pushing me to learn things I would have never thought I could do. If this is for more Web based jobs, I'll be trilled to learn more. If for game development, I would get the joy of learning new ways to code all over again.
